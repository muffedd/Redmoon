Privacy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, company, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to inquiries, send project proposals, and communicate during active engagements. We retain contact information for up to 24 months unless you request deletion.

Cookies & analytics

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We believe in a web that respects your attention.
